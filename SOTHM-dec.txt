A mod that adds an item crafting and a Magic system to AGOT.

Adds some tweaks to how Dragon code behaves, notably, with respect to growth, and Dragon longevity.

Some things may be buggy at the moment. Will be worked on overtime.

If anyone wants to offer some trait icon art, it would be deeply appreciated.

I welcome any suggestions for making the UI more intuitive.

As this mod edits the UI, it is important to load it low in your mod list.

Current Features Magic:
- Adds a large scale magic system, with a total of 8 distinct disciplines of magic.
- Adds a new activity to explore your magical abilities and discover new mysteries.
- Each discipline of magic has at least 17 mysteries.
- All mysteries scale with caster ability
- Uncapped Caster skills, Different masters will have different level of abilities.
- Scroll Creating System lets you make a magic scroll of every known mystery for gold.
- Comprehensive Magic Teaching System, lets you teach additional mages.
- Dynamic Mage usage. You are ability to use mages in your realm to cast mysteries for you.
- Scaling Magical skills. Your mysteries grow in potency based on the respective discipline skill.
- Miscast system, your skill in magic broadly, determines whether or not your magic will miscast and cause the caster harm.

Current Features Item Crafting:
- Create multiple different types of items, with the possibility of enchanting items with various bonuses and modifiers.
- Choose from all of the AGOT swords the cosmetic you want your crafted blade to have.
- Scaling bonuses based on the crafting character's ability in magic.


Most Recent Change
- New UI element to show drops of power in your realm, done by Taelwynn.
